# Discovery Encounter 48: Find Practice Hieroglyph Block

*   **Encounter Name:** Scribe's Practice Stone
*   **Background Story:** While exploring areas associated with learning or craftwork (quarries, mason's yards, scriptoriums/schools in ruins), the NPC finds evidence of training exercises.
*   **Setting Details:** A block of stone (or possibly a plastered board) lies amidst debris, its surface covered in repeated symbols or clumsy attempts at hieroglyphic writing.
*   **Trigger:** Exploring quarries, mason's yards, scriptoriums/schools in ruins.
*   **Effect on NPC:** Finds a stone covered in repeated symbols or clumsy attempts at writing.
*   **NPC Action:** Examine the carvings. *[Potential Skill Check: Archaeology or Literacy]*
*   **Outcome:** Identifies it as a [[encounters-objects/Items/Practice Hieroglyph Block|Practice Hieroglyph Block]] for scribes or carvers. Demonstrates training activities. Little intrinsic value. Minor *[Archaeology]*/*[Literacy]* XP.
*   **Intrigue:** What specific signs were being practiced? Does it show the development of a student's skill?
*   **Combat:** No.
*   **Skill Learned:** Minor *[Archaeology]*/*[Literacy]* XP.
*   **Item Received:** No (block usually too heavy/uninteresting to take).
*   **Player Interaction Point:** No.
*   **Lore Introduced:**
    *   Scribal and carving skills required extensive practice. \[[lore.md]]
    *   Provides insight into Khemri education/apprenticeship. \[[lore.md]]
