# Discovery Encounter 25: Locate Set of Weights and Measures

*   **Encounter Name:** Merchant's Weights
*   **Background Story:** While searching through ruins known for trade or administration, like market areas, merchant houses, or official buildings, the NPC finds tools related to commerce.
*   **Setting Details:** Could be scattered on a counter in a ruined shop, stored in a niche in an administrative office, or found within a buried container.
*   **Trigger:** Exploring ruined market areas, merchant houses, administrative buildings.
*   **Effect on NPC:** Finds a collection of carefully shaped stone or metal weights, possibly alongside a damaged [[encounters-objects/Items/Balance Scale|Balance Scale]].
*   **NPC Action:** Examine the weights. *[Potential Skill Check: Negotiation or Archaeology]*
*   **Outcome:** Recovers a [[encounters-objects/Items/Set of Weights|Set of Weights]]. Minor trade good value. Potential minor *[Negotiation]*/*[Archaeology]* XP if they recognize the weight standard (e.g., deben, kite).
*   **Intrigue:** Do these weights conform to the current standard? Are they unusually fine, suggesting high-status use?
*   **Combat:** No.
*   **Skill Learned:** Minor *[Negotiation]*/*[Archaeology]* XP.
*   **Item Received:** [[encounters-objects/Items/Set of Weights|Set of Weights]] (Trade Good).
*   **Player Interaction Point:** No.
*   **Lore Introduced:**
    *   Standardized weights were used in Khemri trade and administration. \[[lore.md]]
    *   Provides insight into economic practices. \[[lore.md]]
